Breathing Exercises

4-7-8 Technique

A powerful technique to reduce anxiety and promote calm.

  1. 1. Inhale for 4 counts
  2. 2. Hold for 7 counts
  3. 3. Exhale for 8 counts
  4. 4. Repeat 3-4 times
Learn more

Box Breathing

Used by Navy SEALs to stay calm under pressure.

  1. 1. Inhale for 4 counts
  2. 2. Hold for 4 counts
  3. 3. Exhale for 4 counts
  4. 4. Hold for 4 counts

  • • Look for licensed professionals (LCSW, LMFT, PhD, PsyD)

  • • Check if they accept your insurance
  • • Many offer free consultations to see if you're a good fit
